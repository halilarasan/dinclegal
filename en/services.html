Our Legal Services

Home / Services

Across a broad range of legal fields, we offer individual and corporate clients

comprehensive legal consultancy and litigation services.

Legal Counsel

We ensure top-tier legal representation of companies before public authorities, business partners and third parties.

  • Meticulous handling from start to finish of all commercial, administrative and legal proceedings, enforcement actions and disputes brought against or on behalf of companies
  • Drafting of commercial contracts in line with company interests, conducting risk analyses and managing revision processes
  • Proactive identification of legal risks in operational and strategic decisions to prevent loss of rights and disputes

Commercial Law

We provide legal security for businesses in their commercial activities.

  • Company formation and restructuring
  • Partnership disputes
  • Commercial contract drafting and negotiation
  • Competition law consultancy

Mediation

We resolve disputes swiftly through mediation, achieving final outcomes by mutual consent of the parties.

  • Full confidentiality of meetings and information or documents shared by the parties, safeguarding commercial reputation and privacy
  • Establishment of a constructive negotiation environment that bridges the parties
  • Avoiding the exhausting effects of litigation while preserving sustainable commercial and human relations between parties

Labor Law

We produce balanced and effective solutions in employee-employer disputes.

  • Reinstatement cases
  • Severance, notice and overtime compensation
  • Work accident and occupational disease cases
  • Employment contract drafting and review

Real Estate Law

We offer legal security in real estate purchase, sale and rental processes.

  • Title cancellation and registration cases
  • Rental disputes and eviction cases
  • Condominium and zoning law
  • Real estate purchase and sale contracts

Enforcement & Bankruptcy

We provide effective legal support in debt collection and debt management processes.

  • Enforcement proceedings with and without judgment
  • Seizure operations and objections
  • Bankruptcy and concordat processes
  • Debt and promissory note collection

Family Law

We provide sensitive and solution-oriented legal support in family disputes.

  • Consensual and contested divorce cases
  • Custody, visitation and alimony
  • Property regime and liquidation
  • Adoption and guardianship proceedings

Criminal Law

We effectively defend our clients' rights at every stage of criminal proceedings.

  • Defense during investigation and prosecution
  • Detention and judicial control appeals
  • Applications under the Criminal Procedure Code
  • Appeal and cassation applications
