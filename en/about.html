Our Law Firm

A Legal Partnership Built on Trust

As Dinç Legal, we provide comprehensive legal consultancy to numerous domestic and foreign companies from our Altunizade-based office, backed by years of experience. Our core philosophy is not to view legal matters merely as files, but to build long-term and lasting collaborations with our clients based on mutual trust.

Together with our strong solution partners and expert network, we provide the legal support our corporate clients need in their operational and commercial processes from a single center, with high professional care and standards. Our firm, well versed in the dynamics of the business world, manages companies' legal processes with a proactive and preventive approach to legal risks.

We maintain this qualified and long-term consultancy approach offered to corporate structures with the same diligence in individual disputes and litigation requiring personal sensitivity. We regard the corporate discipline we demonstrate in our commercial partnerships as a fundamental principle when protecting the legal rights of our individual clients as well.

Our priority is to conduct all your legal processes within the framework of transparency and professional principles, and to be a long-term legal partner you can rely on in your commercial and personal activities.

Our Founder

Attorney Gökhan Dinç

A graduate of Marmara University Faculty of Law, he continues his professional practice as a registered member of the Istanbul Bar Association.

Our firm embraces a high standard of service in managing legal processes. Alongside our legal work, we carry out joint projects with numerous civil society organizations and contribute to the social advancement of law through various professional memberships. Guided by our core principle of “commitment to principles and trust,” we provide strategic legal support not only to our established domestic clients but also to our foreign business partners.

Under the Dinç Legal umbrella, we build a trust-based legal partnership to simplify your legal processes and manage them with the right strategies.
